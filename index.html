Corpsewood Creature Archive

Stagwalker

Appearance: Medium height, completely black, long stringy hair, small curved deer-like horns, and a single large glowing eye. Hands and feet slightly elongated.

Behavior: Nocturnal and cautious, moves low to the ground, stalks silently, mimics subtle forest sounds, observes intruders from a distance, may vanish instantly if approached.

Habitat: Dense forests, abandoned clearings, and near ruins. Uses hollowed-out areas or thick foliage for temporary shelter.

Lore / Sightings: Reports describe a single glowing eye in darkness. Footprints are distorted, sometimes human-like. Leaves piles of sticks or small markings along its path. Associated with disappearances in the forest.

Caution: Avoid wandering alone at night. Lights or sudden movement may provoke it or cause it to flee.

Eyebringer

Appearance: Scary glowing eyes that pierce the darkness. The bottom of its head looks infected, with exposed flesh and raw tissue visible. Humanoid-ish but grotesque and unsettling.

Behavior: Moves slowly but deliberately. Often waits in dark corners or behind trees, staring with glowing eyes. Can cause fear-induced paralysis in victims.

Habitat: Dark forest floors, abandoned cabins, and areas near swamps or decayed zones in Corpsewood.

Lore / Sightings: Locals report seeing multiple glowing eyes in the forest at once. The infected appearance suggests it may have been hurt or altered somehow. Associated with panic, sudden disappearances, and lingering whispers.

Caution: Never approach. Its gaze is unnerving; flashlights or sudden movement may trigger aggression.
